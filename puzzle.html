Benefits of Chess Puzzles

Tactics are the most important aspect of the game for chess players of all levels, from beginner to grandmaster.
Here you can learn, train, and improve your tactical skills based on positions that happened in real tournament games.

Solving puzzles will not only help you spot them faster during a game, but will also help you learn how to build them.
By training your mind to find hidden tactical ideas in some positions you will develop your imagination and improve your game.
