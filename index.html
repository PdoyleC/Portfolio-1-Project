Crosshaven Chess Society

Welcome

Crosshaven Chess Society was founded in 2016 and has had its share of success over the years in getting to the final stages of a number of competitions in the Munster and Irish leagues. In 2017 the club started doing beginner lessons, and then in 2019 were about to add intermediate & advanced lessons, due to the arrival of Japanese GrandMaster Akio Kobayashi. .
